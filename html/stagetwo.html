STAGE 02

Mission Briefing: Jarvis Command Room

Round 2 Objective: Design the Arc Reactor Energy Distribution Panel — a control interface that allocates reactor power across critical subsystems while maintaining system stability in real time.

Core Task

  • Display total available energy (e.g., 100 units).
  • Show power allocation for multiple subsystems: Propulsion, Defense, Navigation, Communication.
  • Allow users to allocate or reduce power to each subsystem using buttons or input fields.
  • Update remaining energy and subsystem efficiency levels dynamically as allocations change.
  • Implement validation logic that prevents over-allocation beyond the total available energy.

Required Interaction

  • Interactive controls (buttons/inputs) to adjust subsystem power levels.
  • Real-time visual update of energy balance and subsystem status.
  • Clear feedback when allocations are invalid or exceed total energy.
